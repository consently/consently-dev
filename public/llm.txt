# Consently - DPDPA 2023 Consent Manager

## Overview
Consently is India's premier DPDPA 2023 compliant consent management platform. We help Indian businesses achieve data protection compliance through automated cookie scanning and comprehensive consent management.

## Product Information
- **Platform**: DPDPA 2023 Consent Management Platform
- **Target Market**: Indian businesses of all sizes
- **Core Features**: Automated cookie scanning, multilingual consent banners, DPDPA consent handling, real-time analytics
- **Languages Supported**: 22 Indian languages (all Schedule 8 languages)
- **Compliance**: Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023

## Key Features
1. **Automated Cookie Scanning**
   - Quick Scan: Homepage analysis
   - Standard Scan: Top 10 URLs
   - Deep Crawl: 50+ pages
   - Automatic cookie classification
   - Instant compliance reports

2. **DPDPA Consent Management**
   - Pre-loaded templates for various industries
   - Data processing activity management
   - Consent recording with audit trails
   - Withdrawal and preference management

3. **Multilingual Support**
   - All 22 Schedule 8 Indian languages
   - Auto-generated consent banners
   - Regional variations supported

4. **Privacy & Security**
   - Email tokenization
   - Data minimization
   - End-to-end encryption
   - Privacy by design principles

5. **Analytics & Reporting**
   - Real-time dashboards
   - Consent metrics tracking
   - Exportable reports (CSV/JSON/PDF)
   - Complete audit logs

## Pricing Plans
